The Perfect Crêpe

Description

Crêpes are delicious deserts that can be eaten in any flavor you prefer. They are soft and light so enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 - 6 Eggs (depending on how many you are making.)
  • 1/2 a cup of sugar
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 4 cups of milk
  • Lime zest
  • Butter or oil(whichever you prefer)

Directions

  1. Get bowl to mix the batter.
  2. Beat the eggs in the bowl.
  3. Add sugar to the eggs and continue mixing.
  4. Put the flour in the mix.
  5. Pour in the milk and mix all together.
  6. Heat the pan until it is a little hot.
  7. Put the oil/butter into the pan and add the batter in
  8. wait until the batter has changed color on one side before flipping it and doing the same thing to the other side.
